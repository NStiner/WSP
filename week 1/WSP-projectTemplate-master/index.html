    DROP DOWN/FLY OUT MENUS

    WHAT I HOPE TO PRACTICE/LEARN?

    One thing I would like to practice on this month is my drop down/fly out menus. I think these navigational styles always look fantastic and make navigating the page much more easier and simpler than if you were to have a long navigational list. Using these types of components allow you to combine items that are alike, that make it easier for your viewers to find what they need.

    HOW THIS RELATES TO WHAT I HAVE/WILL LEARN IN WDD

    We are learning components in WDD and drop down / flyout menus are a great example of this. They allow creators to give their viewers a more efficient way to navigate throughout the page and makes it easier to find specific content or sections.

    HOW MIGHT I CREATE THIS?

    1. If you're using this component as a navigational system, then you first start out with a '< header >' element.
    2. Next enter in your branding or title by using a heading or img, such as < h1 >, < h2 >, or < h3 >, and < img >
    3. Then you create your navigation items by creating the < nav > tag and placing your < ul >, or main title and then adding < li >'s nested into the < ul >'s to create your drop down content.
